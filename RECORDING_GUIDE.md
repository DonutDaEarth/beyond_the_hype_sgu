## üé¨ RECORDING TIPS

### Before Recording:

1. **Test all demos** - Run every test case successfully
2. **Prepare slides** - Have all PowerPoint slides ready
3. **Script key points** - Write down important quotes
4. **Set up environment** - Good lighting, clear audio, clean background
5. **Have water ready** - Stay hydrated between takes

### During Recording:

1. **Multiple takes are OK** - Redo any section until perfect
2. **Speak clearly** - Enunciate, don't rush
3. **Show enthusiasm** - Energy translates through video
4. **Point to screen** - Guide viewers' attention visually
5. **Pause for effect** - Let important points sink in

### After Recording:

1. **Edit seamlessly** - Cut awkward pauses, technical issues
2. **Add visual aids** - Text overlays, arrows, highlights
3. **Balance audio** - Normalize volume between presenters
4. **Add transitions** - Professional fade-ins/fade-outs
5. **Review timing** - Ensure exactly 30:00 total
